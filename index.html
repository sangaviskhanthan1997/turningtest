The Turing Test
Let's go back to 1951 when the first tests were done to settle machine intelligence, this was called the Turing Test created by Alan Turing

(zoom:2)

HOW does the test work?
  • - Three terminals-physically separated

  • - 2 Humans- one of which are a questioner to the other two participants

  • - 1 Computer- that tries to mimic the responses similar to humans


  • - After a seris of rounds one of the respondents will try to figure which of the three terminals is the computer?
The criticism this test faced

  • -criticized upon the limitation of the questions that are asked

  • - yes and no questions are easy to answer

  • Questions that should be used for this test should challenge the emotional and socailly sensitive topics of these computers
